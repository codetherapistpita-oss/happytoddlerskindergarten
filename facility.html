School Facilities

We nurture holistic development with co-curricular activities including Music, Dance & Drama (MDD), Arts & Crafts, and more.

Safe and reliable van transport for pick-up and drop-off. Our transport facilities ensure your child arrives safely every day.

Our compound is well set and equipped with play equipment for children. A safe outdoor space where kids play, exercise, and develop physical skills.

Healthy meals and nutritious food are provided for all children. What your child eats is carefully planned to support growth and energy throughout the day.

Positive Learning

Engaging classrooms and qualified teachers create a nurturing environment where every child discovers the joy of learning.
