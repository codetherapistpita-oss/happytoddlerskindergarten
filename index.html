About Happy Toddlers Kindergarten & Daycare

Our Vision: To provide a strong foundation that stimulates early learning and child care experiences, promoting excellence in child growth and development.

We welcome children from 1 to 5 years old. Our programs include national curriculum classes, daycare, music, dance, drama, arts, and computer basics. School hours: Monday–Friday, 7:00 AM – 4:00 PM.
